I am an applied microeconomist working on topics in health and labour economics. In my current research, I study childcare as a determinant of fertility choices, maternal health, and health inequalities.

In August 2026 I will be a postdoctoral fellow at the FAIR Institute at the Norwegian School of Economics (NHH).

Before my doctoral studies, I obtained a B.A. in Economics & Management from the University of Oxford and an M.Sc in Economics from University College London. I also worked for the U.K. Government Economic Service on energy price modelling, digital markets, and family related policies.
